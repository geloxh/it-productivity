### LEDGER DESCRIPTION :
- **Excellent / Like New** : No visible scratches, dents, or damage.
Works perfectly. Brand-new machine, no wear and tear, fully
functional.
-**Good** : Minor cosmetic wear (small scratches), fully functional.
Slight marks on casing, keys slightly worn, no performance issues.
-**Fair / Acceptable** : Noticeable wear or cosmetic damage, minor
issues that do not prevent use. Scratched screen bezel, minor dents,
battery not at full capacity.
- **Poor / Damaged** : Major cosmetic damage or functional issues
affecting usability.
Broken keys, cracked screens, slow or failing hardware, require
repair.
- **Non-Functional / Scrap**: Cannot operate without major repairs.
Dead motherboard, severe liquid damage, irreparable components.
